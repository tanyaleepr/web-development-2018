About Us

This page was created by Tanya Lee for the intent of sharing a bit of the Puerto Rican DNA results based on our history, culture, food recipes, language, music,and more. As the Puerto Rican government has taken down our history clasess, this page will also help students to navegate and find facts abour our history. An uncommon knowledge, Puerto Rico was invaded by the Spaniards in 1502. By that time, our native Taínos gave them a warm welcome till the Spaniards tried to kill them all. After unsucessfully trial, the Spaniards brought slaves from Africa and that is how the Coquí DNA was born.

What means "Coquí DNA"?

First of all, the Coquí is the name of our native "frog" and was named like the sound it makes at night. It is an unique speciment in the world that was originated in Puerto Rico. This small frog is smaller than a quarter.

Second, I add the "DNA" part to make it more personal on sharing our Taíno, European, and African roots.
